#StopAsianHate Around The Globe

These are all the #StopAsianHate tweets collected over the course of 24 hours on March 26th, 2021.

Zoom in below to view the content of individual tweets.

Below are a series of maps showing a breakdown of (1) tweets posted from ~4am-10am EST (2) tweets posted from ~10am-4pm EST (3) tweets posted from ~4pm-10pm EST and (4) tweets posted from ~10pm-4am EST over the course of this 24 hour time frame. You can notice (and interactively explore) how different regions of the world tweeted at different frequencies depending on their local time zone, especially in the Asia-Pacific regions.

Below is a histogram of the number of tweets over the course of the 24-hour period above, corrected to correspond to local time and readjusted to display starting at midnight. We can see that, true to intuition, most tweets are posted during waking hours between 9am and 10pm, with a slump in the early morning hours of between 2am and 5am.
